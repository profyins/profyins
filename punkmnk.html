PunkMonk Psychobio Techniques

Techniques created to properly exploit the mind body connection to strengthen and endow abilities greater than the sum of their parts. Through the use of technology and technique One can achieve things that would seem to only be possible through heavy cyber augmentation.

Combining braindance to teach the body at visceral level, Netarchitecture for enhanced speed of learning, and physical training, One can learn lost and new techniques for controlling and enhancing biological performance at speeds which were previously impossible. Balancing the discconect of hyperlearning and foreign knowledge implantation, meditation and physical training can re align the mind body and soul to improve without losing humanity.

Psychoware

These techniques make use of the explosive combination of neurology and biology, and often dont work with cyberware that replaces the biological component to achieve similar effects.

Punk Monk Technique

Psychobio techniques requires a healthy mind, humanity must be at least 50% of your max humanity

After obtaining the appropriate punkmonk technique learning suite, roll a dice based on the difficulty of the technique. This determines how difficult the task will be for each person. At the end of the week, roll against the DV of the training level. if you succeed roll a dice based on the training level, If you fail time is wasted and you must start again subtract this number from the difficulty of technique roll. When that number equals zero, you have learned this technique. If its a psychoware technique, it uses a cyberware slot, and is mutually exclusive with some cyberware.with similar properties

PunkMonk training roll =1d10 + concentration+will + one half your highest martial art ability)
